Pilau Recipe

Pilau Dish

This is a tasty Indian side dish made easily and quickly in the Instant Pot

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/4 cup diced red onion
  • 3/4 tablesppon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 garlic onio

Steps for it's Preparation

  • 1. Heat the oil in a cooking pot and add the red onions to cook until brown.
  • 2. Add garlic
  • 3. Add Pilau Masala and let it cook until it starts to smoke. At this point add the beef and let it cook for one minute
  • 4. Next add the tomatoes and stir until it forms a thick mixture
  • 5. At this point, crush the 3 Royco cubes into the mixture and stir to ensure they are absorbed into the beef
  • 6. Add salt to taste and then add water and let it come to a boil.
  • 7. Wash the rice and then add to the mixture and stir.
  • 8. Let it cook for 15-20 minutes or until the rice is soft.
